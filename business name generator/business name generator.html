Professional

A business name should convey a sense of professionalism and credibility. This can be achieved through the use of formal language or by avoiding slang or colloquial terms.

Relevance

A good business name should be relevant to the products or services offered by the business. This helps to establish brand identity and can make it easier for customers to understand what the business does.

Memorable

A business name should be easy to remember so that customers can recall it and refer it to others. This can be achieved through the use of catchy or unique words, or by incorporating elements of the business's branding or identity.

Why a marked name?

For new organizations, naming choices can appear to be very restricted. Short spaces are extravagant, yet longer multi-word names don't rouse certainty. In 2022 numerous new businesses are picking a short, marked name - a name that is exceptional, critical and reasonable.
